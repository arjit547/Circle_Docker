Introduction

CircleCI is a continuous integration and continuous delivery (CI/CD) platform that automates the building, testing, and deployment of software applications. It is commonly used by software development teams to streamline their development process, increase code quality, and ensure that changes to the codebase can be quickly and safely deployed to production.
